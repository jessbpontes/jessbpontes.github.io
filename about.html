About me

I'm Jess, Capricorn, 28, Brazilian, Product Designer driven by knowledge and passion.

Jessica Pontes

I am a Brazilian designer with a passion for solving complex problems and creating solutions that people fall in love with. My expertise bridges the gap between design thinking and business strategy.

With a Master's Degree in Design Management, I bring a unique perspective to product development. I have a strong background in the finance market, SaaS, mobile, and enterprise solutions, helping others understand the intersection of design and business value.

My professional journey has taken me through diverse industries, where I've consistently transformed user pain points into intuitive experiences that drive adoption and growth.

I find joy in simplicity and extraordinary; I believe that the best products should be deeply connected with people, and culture and that aesthetics is an important part of usability.

I am excited by opportunities to take initiative and work on highly leveraged projects that pose challenges and require growth. I excel at uncovering user needs through user research, defining product and design strategies, and transforming them into products that deliver great user experiences. And I always strive to bring my knowledge from other domains into my designs. Finally, I'm open to opportunities for freelance or artistic collaboration. Don't be afraid to get in touch!
