About me

I'm Jess, Capricorn, 24, Brazilian, UX/UI Designer driven by knowledge and passion.

Jessica Pontes

I am a Brazilian girl with a passion for solving problems and making people fall in love with new solutions.

Currently, I work as a Product Designer at Aubay and pursuing a Master's Degree in Design Management.

In my free time, I like to try different types of food, aquatic sports, or spend time with friends.



I find joy in simplicity and extraordinary; I believe that the best products should be deeply connected with people, culture and that aesthetics is an important part of usability.

I am excited by opportunities to take initiatives and working on highly leveraged projects that pose challenges and require growth. I excel at uncovering user needs through user research, defining product and design strategies, and transforming them into products that deliver great user experiences. And I always strive to bring my knowledge from other domains into my designs. Finally, I'm open to opportunities for freelance or full-time work, artistic collaboration, or having a coffee or lunch. Don't be afraid to get in touch!
