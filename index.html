About Me

A restless designer-in-progress with a new-born fondness for management and strategy.
I've been investigating the discipline of user experience in digital products for the last two years. Half of that time was dedicated to working as a visual designer at leading companies in Brazil, where I had the opportunity to understand user research better, work with fantastic tech professionals, and help to develop their design system and products.
I'm Brazilian, currently living in Lisbon to study a master's. I'm an "on board" person to almost everything. I like chatting, beer and good music. You can find me very focused or in sleeping walking mode too. Anyway, would be a pleaser to meet you.
