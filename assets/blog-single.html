Lift Seller

Register a new customer and buy Liftpay products from anywhere

The context

The Liftpay team worked as a side business of the Liftbank company. Building the products in collaboration with many partners our team was responsable for all that concerned payment solutions.

Lift Seller is one of the products linked to the Lift Pay payment solution, which aims to make entrepreneurs’ lives easier by using technology for a fluid experience. This product was designed for the exclusive use of Liftpay registered sellers in order to improve the number of sellings and for registering customers quickly and securely.

The challenge

In this scenario there were 3 problems

Decrease of sales: Digital campaigns were not as effective in the target audience as expected. It was observed that our user was much more susceptible to the face-to-face sales model. This discovery led to the hiring of sellers but their performance had some requirements.

Negotiate rates: One of the strengths of Lift Pay was the low negotiable rates depending on the type of business. But manage this personable fees for all clients was getting too complicated

Register customers quickly and safely: An efficient registration flow, promotes a good experience when it is fast and clear. However, in the business scenario, a minimum amount of information is necessary and expected to guarantee the safety of the client and the company.
