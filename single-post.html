What is Minimal Theme?

Thought of the day: What have you been doing for your dreams today? 2020 would be the year for me. I had travel plans and specializations, but everything was canceled by the covid-19. Anyone who knows me knows that for some time I was looking for an opportunity to do a master's degree abroad. And so, in the middle of the pandemic, when hope was already at its limit, I received incredible news! And with several borders closed around the world, I decided to embark for Portugal to start a master's degree. The decision alone was not easy, as it involved giving up several things ..., but there is no perfect moment, right? After several stones appeared in the planning, it seemed that everything was going to go wrong, having problems with documents, the increase in the euro, with the covid test, and only embarking 3 months after the scheduled date, I finally arrived in Lisbon. And guess what? State of calamity, a blockade has been decreed and my classes are online. It is true that the problems are not over, but the feeling of looking for what I want is the best I have ever had and I am sure I will never regret trying. I decided to write this here to remind you that your dreams are in your hand, and there is no perfect time to run after them.
